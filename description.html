Position at Funding Circle

At Funding Circle there’s a mission that drives everything we do. Around the world, we want to help small businesses get the finance they need to thrive, creating thousands of jobs as they drive the economy forward.

With teams in London, San Francisco, Amsterdam and Berlin, we’re fusing finance and tech to create new opportunities for those who want to achieve more.

As the UWA Team Lead on our team at Funding Circle you get to…

The Underwriting Analyst (UWA) Team Lead supervises a specific team of Analysts and/or Assistants to complete the deal review process following established procedures and ruleset. The position reports to the UWA Manager and is responsible for daily production for the team. The Team lead is also responsible for educating the team members and ensure the team is up to speed on procedure and policy changes.

You’ve been there and done this...

  • Supervised a specific sub-team in UWA
  • Monitored workload and individual production - set daily goals for team
  • Coached team members on production numbers
  • Assured team members are fully educated on new procedures and policy changes
  • Worked with department manager to write and update procedures
  • Completed Peer reviews on each team member on regular schedule
  • Onboarded new joiners
  • Assisted the Department Manager in preparation of performance reviews
  • Involved team on various projects as needed
  • Assisted Manager with department audits
  • Prepared and ran production reports looking for follow up opportunities and provide on the spot coaching
  • Attended funnel, product or other project meetings as directed by manager

This is an opportunity to join one of FC USA's core functions and gain broad exposure to both the company and the alternative lending industry. You will be working within a dynamic and high-performing team to help take FC USA to the next level!

It would be great if you also have…

  • High level of professionalism, positivity, and enthusiasm for the role and for Funding Circle
  • Experience in performance management
  • 2- 3 years experience managing or supervising others
  • Knowledge of basic financial accounting
  • Metrics-driven and analytical approach to problem solving
  • Skilled at operations management
  • Strong interpersonal skills with a high level of self-awareness and empathy
  • Experience with credit operations in banking, credit card or loan processing a plus
  • Alignment with FC USA's core values: Think Smart, Make it Happen, Be Open, Stand Together and Live the Adventure

Why join us?

Happy employees are productive employees, that’s why we offer a hearty benefits package. From learning and development and commuter stipends, to a competitive salary, equity, and health benefits, we’ve got you covered! That being said, have you heard about what we're doing?! Our mission is what really motivates us to come to work each day:

  •     We're supporting small business, the engine of economic growth
  •     We're helping facilitate higher yields for investors and lower interest rates for borrowers
  •     We can fund loans extremely quickly, all online!
  •     We have a clear competitive advantage globally in areas like domain expertise and regulatory processes
